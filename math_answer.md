# Solving 45 × 28

Here's my step-by-step work following the teacher's hints:

## Step 1: Break it down using the distributive property
**45 × 28 = 45 × (20 + 8)**

## Step 2: Multiply 45 × 20
45 × 20 = **900**

## Step 3: Multiply 45 × 8
45 × 8 = **360**

## Step 4: Add the two results together
900 + 360 = **1,260**

---

## ✅ Final Answer: **45 × 28 = 1,260**
